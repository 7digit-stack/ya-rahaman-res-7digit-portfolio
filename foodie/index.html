Ya Rahman Biriyani Tradition in Every Grain

Since our first handi went on the dum flame, Ya Rahman Biriyani has stayed true to one promise — authentic Hyderabadi taste, slow-cooked the traditional way. Every biriyani is layered by hand, sealed with dough, and cooked low and slow until rice, meat and spice become one.
